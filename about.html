Who We Are

The Prisoner Skills project is a group of Full Stack Web Developers.
We all have different levels of development skills, and enjoy building awsome web projects together.

Why We Do This

Our group wants to change peoples lives for the better. We chose this project to bring awareness to the difficult situation prisoners & parolees face daily. So many want to learn skills & work, but the opportunities for them are rare.
Hopefully, this will someday soon be changed

Everyone deserves a second chance!
