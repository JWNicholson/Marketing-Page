Why use us?

Incarcerated adults sometimes have a hard time re-entering society. Unemployment due to lack of skills or finding a willing employer is difficult. Unfortunately, this leads many to re-offend and return to the correctional system.

Prisoner Skills is preventing those situations for many offendors. We found lower levels of education and a lack of vocational skills adds to the repeat offender problem.

Inmates recieving training and the opportunity to earn while in-house or on furlough find their outlook on life change for the better. That's why Prisoner Skills helps match institutions & inmates with employers.

If you are a penal instition with a work training program, sign up and truly help reform your inmates.

If you are an employer, sign up and browse through our list of eager workers.
